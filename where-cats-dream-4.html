Where Cats Dream

Somewhere between sunlight, flowers, and stars, a small black cat is wandering. Stay a while and see where she leads you.

How to visit

  • 🐾
    Touch her — tap or click the cat anywhere. She might dart away startled, or lean in and purr. Her mood is different every time.
  • ☀️
    Move the sunlight — drag the glowing patch in the Sunbeam Room. She'll wander toward it and may nap there.
  • 🌸
    Plant a flower, make a wish — in the Garden, pick a bloom and tap the grass. You'll be asked to leave a little wish with it — everything is remembered.
  • 💤
    Wait quietly — if you leave the garden idle, she'll curl up and a dream bubble will rise above her. Tap it to enter the sky.
  • ✨
    Hunt stars together — every flower becomes a star. She flies alongside you — tap a star to catch it, then leave your name and a message written in light.
  • 🐾
    Walk with her — in the Park, you're walking her on a leash, watching her glance up at you now and then.
  • 💬
    Talk to her — the chat bubble in the corner lets you say anything. She's a cat, though — expect a moody, honest reply.
